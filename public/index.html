How to Use & Mappings

🚀 Quick Start

  1. Type your target absolute workspace path in the top input bar (e.g. /home/user/ros2_ws) and click Scan & Sync.
  2. Click the main Play Codebase button to start the music.
  3. Tune individual volume channels (crackle, chords, bass, melody, drums) in the Sound Mixer card.

✨ Gemini AI Mode

  • Enter your Gemini API Key in the settings panel and check the AI Compose toggle.
  • Gemini will read your codebase statistics and generate dynamic jazzy or moody chords, scales, and tempos based on the code's character.

🎵 Mappings Summary

  • Lines of Code (LOC) ➜ Tempo (BPM)
  • C++ Density ➜ Moody D Minor scale
  • Python/JS Density ➜ Warm F Major scale
  • Web (HTML/CSS) Density ➜ High-frequency pad brightness
